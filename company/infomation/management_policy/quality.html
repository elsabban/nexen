THE QUALITY WARRANTY SYSTEM ONLY OFFERED BY NEXEN TIRE
NEXEN is an advanced business that pursues customer’s impression based on the consistent quality innovation activities as a way to secure the top quality products.
The quality policy of NEXEN TIRE Co., Ltd. is as shown below. To realize such objectives, the quality goals shall be set and reflected to the management plan and all employees shall exert utmost efforts for the achievement of such goals. In this extent, NEXEN TIRE has secured the quality system that is suitable for ISO/TS16949 and Product Liability; thus, the CEO declares to supply only the products that satisfy the customers and to observe the environmental laws through the consistent research and development based on the safety and reliability of the products. All the organizations and board members that are interested in such quality management system shall observe the following.
  • 1. Active acceptance of the customers’ requests
  • 2. Improvement of competitiveness by eliminating the loss in the process of planning and production
  • 3. Improvement of quality level through continuous improvement activities
For the effective operation and continuous maintenance/improvement of the quality system throughout the production activity for all products, the CEO shall appoint a deputy of quality management to ensure the practice and maintenance of requested issues regarding the quality system, having the overall responsibility and authority on the management of the human and material resource while exerting the utmost efforts for the consistent improvement of the quality-related issues as the CEO.
NEXEN TIRE Co., Ltd. CEO Travis Kang
NEXEN TIRE THAT PRACTICES THE ENVIRONMENT SAFETY
NEXEN TIRE promises to create an advanced business image by practicing the following: Waste reduction by planning environment-friendly products stressing the importance in environmental management and minimization of environmental contamination in the production activities; Security of an environment-friendly business image through the saving of resources;
Observation of the laws and regulations required for the realization of zero-accident and zero-disease in the workplace based on autonomous practice and active participation of the employees; Consistent improvement of safety health. The environment policy of NEXEN TIRE Co., Ltd. is as shown below. To realize such objectives, the environment goals shall be set and reflected to the management plan and all employees shall exert utmost efforts for the achievement of such goals. In this extent, NEXEN TIRE has secured the environment system that is suitable for ISO 14001, ISO45001 and KOSHA-MS thus, the CEO declares to supply only the products that are safe and reliable. All the organizations and board members that are interested in such environment management system shall observe the following.
  • 1. Construction of the environment management system and the maintenance and continuous improvement of such system
  • 2. Observation of the domestic and international laws as well as other regulations concerning the environment
  • 3. Security of an advanced business through consistent risk improvement
For the effective operation and continuous maintenance/improvement of the environmental/safety health throughout the production activity for all products, the CEO shall appoint a deputy for the management of environmental/safety health to ensure the practice and maintenance of the requested issues regarding environment/safety health, having the overall responsibility and authority on the management of the human and material resource while exerting the utmost efforts for the consistent improvement of the environmental/safety health-related issue as the CEO
NEXEN TIRE Co., Ltd. CEO Travis Kang
SAFETY POLICY OF IMPORT AND EXPORT
The legal regulations shall be consistently observed and improved to ensure the safety of import/export and distribution while securing the business image as a leading and safe distribution and supply network by sharing such regulations with the interested parties. Hence, such measures are established for the safe import and export method of NEXEN TIRE Co., Ltd.
To realize this, all employees shall exert utmost efforts aiming at the effective implementation of the management plan and achievement of the goal as set for the safe import and export objectives. Thus, the Import/Export Safety Management System of the company secured a system that satisfies the AEO of Korea Customs Service and in this extent, the company’s CEO declares the safe supply of the products that satisfy the customers and that comply with the legal regulations on import/export and customs based on product stability, reliability, and accumulated technology. All the organizations and employees related to the Import/Export Safety Management System shall observe the following items.
  • 1. Construction of the Import/Export Safety Management System and its continuous improvement
  • 2. Compliance with the legal regulations related to the domestic and international Import/Export Safety Management System and other requirements
  • 3. Security of transparency through the disclosure of the measures concerning import/export safety as well as other necessary information
CEO shall assume the general responsibility and authority over the management of human and physical resources for the smooth operation, consistent maintenance, and continuous improvement of the Import/Export Safety Management System, aiming at the manufacture activities of all products, while carefully selecting the general manager of AEO among the managers of the organizations to effectively secure the practice and maintenance of requirements concerning the Import/Export Safety Management System. As CEO of NEXEN TIRE, I promise to continuously exert my utmost efforts on the matters related to Import/Export safety.
NEXEN TIRE Co., Ltd. CEO Travis Kang
