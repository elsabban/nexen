Report Guide
We receive reports on any conduct that violates or has the potential to violate the Code of Ethics, such as employee's job-related irregularities(Fraud), damage to the company image, and damage to employee reputation. In this case, we will investigate the facts on the premise of thorough confidentiality of the identity of the informant, and the contents of the report, so as not to receive any disadvantages due to the report.
1. Scope of report
We receive reports on violations of the company's Code of Ethics.
  • Business-related money and goods, treat/entertainment, and convenience
  • Interference with fair performance of duties due to mediation, solicitation, etc.
  • Acceptance of unfair profits, breach of trust, embezzlement, etc. using position.
  • Other acts that violate NEXEN TIRE's Code of Ethics, etc.
2. How to report
You can choose a method that is convenient for the informant to report.
The details of the report should be described in detail according to the five W's and one H, and if you have relevant evidence, please attach it together.
  • Homepage : Click the <Report> button at the top
  • Email : nextenethicscenter@gmail.com
  • Letter : Ethics Management Team on the 7th floor of The Nexen Univercity, 177, Magokjungang-ro, Gangseo-gu, Seoul, Republic of Korea
Informant Protection Program
In accordance with Article 15 of the Rules for operation of the Code of Ethics, we operate the informant protection program so that informants can report with confidence from any disadvantages or unfair treatment.
1. Principle of protection.
  • The identity of the informant, the details of the report, and the result of processing are kept strictly confidential so that they are not disclosed against the will of the informant.
  • There is no disadvantage or discrimination in status for legitimate reports.
2. How to report
  • The reporting system is operated under a safe security system, and the department in charge of ethics management will take actionable measures to prevent any disadvantages due to reporting.
  • If the informant is a party involved in a violation of the Code of Ethics or illegal acts, the liability may be reduced.
  • After reviewing the report, we will investigate according to NEXEN TIRE's procedures and regulations.
