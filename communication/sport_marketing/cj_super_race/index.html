COMMUNICATION

Sports Marketing Experience the passion of NEXEN TIRE through NEXEN Sports that are shared with the customers.
CJ SUPERRACE

NEXEN TIRE became an official partnership with CJ SUPERRACE Championship, the biggest racing event in Korea .

NEXEN TIRE has established a three-year agreement of official tire supplier for BMW M Class, the first BMW M one-make race in the world launched in 2018. 

The race will show dynamic competition of BMW M4 coupe and all the vehicle will equip NEXEN TIRE’s N’FERA SUR4G

Together with its success of NEXEN TIRE Speed racing and Formula Drift, again, NEXEN TIRE will prove its product performance of high grip semi-slick tire, N’FERA SUR4G.

If you wish to find out about the class formation and schedule, check the official website of SUPERRACE. You can view all kinds of information.
